Body Mass Index (BMI)

BMI is a simple index of weight-for-height that is commonly used to classify underweight, overweight, and obesity in adults.

Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR)

BMR is the number of calories required to keep your body functioning at rest. It's a useful measure to understand how many calories you need daily.
